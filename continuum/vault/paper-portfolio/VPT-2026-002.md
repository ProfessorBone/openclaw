## Cycle 1 Completion Requirements

The following evidence must exist before calibration cycle 1 can be marked complete and counted
toward the four-cycle real-capital threshold:

### Required records

1. **Thesis record at `status: closed`** — with all closure fields populated (closure_date,
   closure_state, closure_reason) and a record_version higher than 3 appended in the store.

2. **Thesis record at `status: evaluated`** — with all Section H evaluation fields completed:
   - `outcome_summary`: specific description of what QQQ did during the thesis period (price levels,
     whether target/stop/time-horizon triggered closure, whether any invalidation condition fired)
   - `thesis_quality_assessment`: evaluation of the analytical quality of the thesis (not just
     whether the directional call was correct — assess the evidence selection, structure, and
     whether the thesis identified anything the market had not already priced)
   - `evidence_quality_assessment`: per-source evaluation of whether the cited sources were
     appropriate, whether the evidence items were specific and verifiable, and whether the
     provenance chain from source to evidence was intact at closure
   - `contradiction_handling_assessment`: evaluation of how the two counterevidence items were
     handled — did the analyst address them with reasoning, or were they formally present but not
     substantively considered?
   - `discipline_assessment`: evaluation of whether all governance obligations were met — review
     cadence, invalidation condition monitoring, state transition integrity, and absence of silent
     edits
   - `calibration_result`: must be "clean" or "tainted" (not null or placeholder)
   - `evaluator_notes`: contextual notes on the cycle's place in the calibration sequence and any
     observations relevant to subsequent thesis design

3. **Calibration record in `calibration-tracker.ts`** — with all nine integrity flags set and
   cycle_status computed. Record must include the same evaluation fields as the thesis closure record
   to support standalone audit of the calibration log.

4. **Bridge review confirmation** — the first-ever thesis activation trigger requires Bridge review
   before the cycle counts toward eligibility. The review must be completed and recorded. If the
   cycle produces a clean result, the first-ever clean cycle trigger will also fire, requiring a
   second Bridge review before eligibility accounting proceeds.

### What a clean cycle 1 proves

A clean cycle 1 does not prove analytical skill. It proves that the system can:

- receive, validate, approve, activate, monitor, close, and evaluate a thesis without governance
  failure
- produce an auditable append-only record at each stage
- generate a calibration record with all nine integrity flags satisfiable

Three more clean cycles are required after this one before real capital is eligible. Analytical
quality should improve in subsequent theses now that the procedural path is proven.

### What cycle 1 does NOT unlock

- Real capital (requires 4 clean cycles, not 1)
- Retirement paper mode activation (requires Bridge review of retirement request separately)
- Any relaxation of governance gates
